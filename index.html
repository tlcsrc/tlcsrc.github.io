TLC SRC

Built with TLC

The Systems Research Centre1 that takes computational trinitarianism seriously—the triplet of Types, Logic ε̷ Categories.

Ultimately, we want to build elegant software systems that are reliable, maintainable, and composable—as well as being fun to write! We resist the temptation to compromise the principles of TLC in order to pursue high performance2.

Please find a list of current projects here.

Higher Type Theory

We plan to design and implement a programming language based on the Univalent Foundations and demonstrate it’s practicality by building principled, dependable software systems. Along the way, we will see how computational trinitarianism is generalised in light of Homotopy Type Theory.

Join us

Whether you’re a programmer, logician or mathematician, we have a flavour of TLC for you! We’d also like to spread a little tender loving care—we believe that when you care about usability3, you inevitably care about TLC.

We welcome collaboration with researchers, programmers, autodidacts, polymaths—you! To get started, simply join the chat, mailing list or email .
