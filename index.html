The Systems Research Centre1 that practises computational trinitarianism:

We build software systems that are elegant, reliable, maintainable. We resist the temptation to compromise our principles in order to pursue high performance2.

We love to work, learn, and collaborate with entrepreneurs, programmers, researchers, and autodidacts.
