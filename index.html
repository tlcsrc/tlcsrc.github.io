TLC SRC

Built with TLC

The Systems Research Centre1 that takes computational trinitarianism seriously—the triplet of Types, Logic & Categories.

Ultimately, we want to build elegant software systems that are reliable, maintainable, and composable—as well as being fun to write! We resist the temptation to compromise the principles of TLC in order to pursue high performance2.

Join us

We welcome discussion with researchers, programmers, autodidacts—you! Simply join the chat, mailing list or email src@steshaw.org.

  1. Named in homage to the original SRC, DEC SRC.↩

  2. We do like low-level bit-twiddling tricks, it’s just that we prefer to get things right before we make them fast.
